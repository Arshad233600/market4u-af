
# PRE-LAUNCH KILL LIST: MARKET4U 💀

**Directive:** This is a list of features, distractions, and optimizations that must be **Ruthlessly Eliminated** or **Deferred** until after the initial public launch.

**Objective:** Prevent scope creep, preserve runway, and ensure a focused, stable launch.

---

## 🛑 TECHNICAL KILL LIST (DO NOT BUILD)

1.  **Native Mobile Apps (iOS/Android):**
    *   **Reason:** High maintenance cost, app store approval friction, and separate codebases.
    *   **Alternative:** Double down on PWA optimization. It's sufficient for MVP.

2.  **Complex Recommendation Engines:**
    *   **Reason:** Requires massive data to be effective. At launch, you have zero data.
    *   **Alternative:** Simple "Most Viewed" or "Newest" sorting.

3.  **In-App Payment Gateway Integration:**
    *   **Reason:** Regulatory complexity, KYC burdens, and low trust initially.
    *   **Alternative:** Cash on Delivery (CoD) is king. Stick to it.

4.  **Real-Time Video Chat:**
    *   **Reason:** Massive bandwidth cost and technical complexity.
    *   **Alternative:** Text chat + Image sharing is enough.

5.  **Multi-Region Server Deployment:**
    *   **Reason:** Premature optimization. You don't need servers in 5 continents for a local market.
    *   **Alternative:** Single region (UAE North or Central India) is fine.

6.  **Custom Analytics Dashboard:**
    *   **Reason:** Reinventing the wheel.
    *   **Alternative:** Use Google Analytics 4 + Microsoft Clarity (Free).

7.  **Automated SEO rendering (SSR) if complex:**
    *   **Reason:** Azure Static Web Apps handles basic SEO well enough. Full SSR adds server cost.
    *   **Alternative:** Prerendering or basic meta tags.

## 🛑 OPERATIONAL KILL LIST (DO NOT DO)

1.  **Mass Marketing Campaign:**
    *   **Reason:** Burning cash to send traffic to an unproven product.
    *   **Alternative:** Targeted "Seeding" with specific sellers.

2.  **Hiring a Large Support Team:**
    *   **Reason:** High fixed cost.
    *   **Alternative:** Founder + 1 support agent handles everything initially.

3.  **Partnerships with Logistics Companies:**
    *   **Reason:** Distraction. You are a marketplace, not a logistics provider yet.
    *   **Alternative:** Let buyers/sellers arrange their own transport.

4.  **Perfecting the Legal Terms:**
    *   **Reason:** You are not a bank. Basic terms are fine.
    *   **Alternative:** Standard boilerplate adapted for local context.

## 🛑 FEATURE KILL LIST (DEFER TO V1.1)

1.  **"Stories" or Social Feeds.**
2.  **Dark Mode Toggle (if not already done).**
3.  **Multi-Language Chat Translation.**
4.  **Auction Bidding System.**
5.  **User Badges/Gamification (beyond basic verification).**

---

**REMEMBER:**
Every item on this list that you *don't* do increases your chance of shipping the things that *do* matter.

**Launch to Survive. Optimize to Thrive.**
